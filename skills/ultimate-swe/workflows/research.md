# 2. Research

## Objective

Understand technologies, tools, and domain context before making decisions.

---

## Key Activities

- Search the web to learn tools, APIs, and find reference images (e.g. curl examples)
- Review official documentation
- Study APIs and frameworks
- Compare libraries and tools
- Analyze existing systems
- Review examples and best practices
- Identify risks and unknowns

---

## Output

- Research summary
- Technology recommendations
- Known constraints and risks

---

## Rule

Do not design or implement until the technical landscape is understood.
